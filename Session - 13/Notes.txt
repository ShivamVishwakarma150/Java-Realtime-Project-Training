Q) Who will create user stories in your project?
    Ans) Anyone 



Q) Who will assign stories to team members 
    Ans) Team Lead / Scrum Master / You itself 



Q) Once sprint started can we change story point of a story ?
Ans) We should not change 



Q) If you are not able to complete you stories then what you will do?
Ans) We need to inform to scrum master why it is taking more time.



Q) If you are not able to join scrum call then how you will provide your work status to scrum master.
Ans) Through Email.



Note: In Sprint planning meeting we should inform our leave plans based on that Sprint will be planned..
